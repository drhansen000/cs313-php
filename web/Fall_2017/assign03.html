The Reason Why

My wife and I love to make crafts! It's a fun and wholesome activity that allows us to spend time together and do something constructive. I decided to make this website as a way that people can share ideas with each other that will help bring them closer to each other. So, if you want to see some of the ideas that my wife and I have done and loved doing, please click the link to any working page! If you have any ideas of your own, please fill out the form below. It can be about different crafting ideas, ideas how to make this website even better, or just general comments. Have fun and let's get crafting!
