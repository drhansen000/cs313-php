Student Focused Learning

Here at Science Tech Univesity, our number one concern is the success of our students. We know how important it is to their future that they do well in school and are prepared to join the workforce and forge the future! Because of this, we offer a variety of options to our students, including one-on-one peer tutors, online resources, and teachers who care.

Networking

One of the greatest challenges that graduates face after college is getting their foot in the door at a company. We offer expertly trained mentors who will help them to learn how to develop a resume, network, get themselves noticed, and pass those interviews! We're here to help every step of the way.

The Library

Library Tools

Here at our library we have over 50,000 books, and it keeps growing! Not only that, but students can feel welcome to use our secure computers to research anything they need. We're here to help and our friendly staff will see to it that our students find what they're looking for!
