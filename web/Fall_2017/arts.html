The Arts

Our University offers many courses that are targeted towards art majors. We want to help shape our future and its culture and we know that our young, bright students are just the ones to do it.

What we Offer

We have a large variety of art-based classes, such as photography, painting, drawing, and much more! Be ready to have a hands-on teaching experience as our teachers help you stretch your artistic abilities. Be prepared to showcase your work to your peers and others in the field, as professionals come from all over the world to help and give their critics and help you build connections!
